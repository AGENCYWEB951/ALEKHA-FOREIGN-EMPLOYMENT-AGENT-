A. B2B PARTNER PORTAL & PROCESS

Step 1

Agency Partnership & Attestation

Verify the Gulf agency/sponsor via Ethiopian Consulates. Upload Demand Letters onto the E-LMIS system.

Step 2

Candidate Sourcing & Registration

Source candidates legally to register in the national system. Minimum deployment age: 25 years old.

Step 3

Medical Clearance & TVET Training

Candidates undergo GAMCA medical exams and mandatory 21-day pre-departure training to obtain a CoC.

Step 4

Visa Stamping & Flight Booking

Process visas via Tasheer / Enjaz / MoFA. Book travel exclusively through Ethiopian Airlines as mandated.
